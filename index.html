Why you should choose Evpay

EvPay by EvMak provides a complete suite of digital payment solutions designed for businesses in Tanzania — enabling fast, secure, and flexible payment collection across mobile money, banks, cards, and more.

Mobile Money Payments

Accept fast and secure payments from all major mobile money platforms including M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, and Halopesa.

Bank Transfer Payments

Seamless bank integrations allowing customers to pay directly from over 30 supported Tanzanian banks.

Card Payments

Accept Visa, Mastercard, and other major cards with secure 3D authentication and real-time confirmation.

QR Code Payments

Generate dynamic or static QR codes to allow quick, app-based payments at physical or digital points of sale.

USSD Push Payments

Enable customers to pay instantly using USSD on any mobile phone — no internet required, making payments accessible to all.

API & System Integration

Developer-friendly APIs and plugins for integrating payments into mobile apps, websites, POS systems, and ERP platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions About EVPay

Here are the most common questions about EVPay Tanzania — how it works, payments, EV charging, and more.

Can I start without a developer?

Yes. Use Payment Links and digital Invoices to go live today. How fast do I see funds? Based on channel SLAs. You’ll see real-time status and settlement views in your dashboard.

How do you handle returns?

We provide return codes and webhook events so you can trigger retries or alternative routes.

Can we test before going live?

Yes. Sandbox accounts, sample batches, and simulated return codes. Before accepting real payments, you can use a sandbox environment to simulate transactions. This allows your team to test payment flows, batch processing, and handling of return codes safely without affecting real funds.

Is Tap to Phone live today?

On the roadmap. We run pilots and can enable cohorts by request. Can we combine cards with other rails at checkout? Yes. Hosted checkout presents multiple rails to your customer.

How do you handle returns?

We provide return codes and webhook events so you can trigger retries or alternative routes. failed or returned payments are communicated to your system via return codes. Webhooks allow automatic handling, ensuring minimal disruption and faster resolution for your customers.

Do you support approvals for large batches?

Yes. Multi-step approvals with thresholds and alerts. For large transactions or batch payments, EVPay allows multiple approval steps to ensure security. You can set thresholds that require additional approvals and receive alerts if an action is needed, reducing risk of errors or fraud.
